About Me

Hello! I’m David :)

Right now (August 2021) I’m an ‘Information Analyst’ at Public Health Scotland. I work in a team that is transforming the way reports are published - we make them reproducible. My team also helps maintain the open data portal found here: https://www.opendata.nhs.scot/.

I used to be a researcher at Emerald Works (which has been rebranded as “Mind Tools for Business”), where I did things like data analysis using, writing and designing interactive research reports using D3.js, and assisting with the development of a product (a benchmarking tool) with an agile team.

Before that, I was at the University of Glasgow studying for a BSc and an MSc. My research projects concerned psychometrics, investigating the measurement of the perceived control (i.e., motivation, self-efficacy, locus of control), and scale ‘validation’.

If you want to contact me, you can @/DM me on Twitter and my personal email is: davidjaikman@outlook.com.
