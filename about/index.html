About Me

Hello! I’m David :)

I’m a researcher at Emerald Works, where the goal of my work is to make methodological advancements in measuring the impact of learning at the organisational and individual level. But I do a bunch of stuff day-to-day, like data analysis using R, writing and designing interactive research reports using D3.js, and assisting with the development of a product (a benchmarking tool) with an agile team.

Previously, I was at the University of Glasgow studying for a BSc and an MSc. My research projects concerned psychometrics, investigating the measurement of the perceived control (i.e., motivation, self-efficacy, locus of control), and scale ‘validation’.

If you want to contact me, you can @/DM me on Twitter and my personal email is: davidjaikman@outlook.com. If you’re especially feeling professional, you could use my work email: daikman@emerald.com.
